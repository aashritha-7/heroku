EMPLOYEE ATTRITION PREDICTOR

Attrition is the silent killer that can switly disable even the most successful and stable organizations in a shockingly spare amount of time. Hiring new employees is an extremely complex task that requires additional investment of capital, time and skills. A Machine Learning model which predicts employee attrition based upon certain factors such as an individual's average working hours, salary level etc is very benificial, thus the model. This makes it easy to analyze the attrition process, make a few required changes and improve the performance of employees.
